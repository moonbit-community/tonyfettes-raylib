# Skyforge Mining Convoy 2026

A twin-stick shooter where you pilot a ship escorting a mining convoy through hostile territory. Collect ore from defeated enemies, protect the convoy trucks, and use an overdrive ability to clear waves.

## Build and Run

```bash
cd examples && moon build --target native raylib_skyforge_mining_convoy_2026/
cd examples && ./_build/native/debug/build/raylib_skyforge_mining_convoy_2026/raylib_skyforge_mining_convoy_2026.exe
```

## Controls

- **WASD**: Move the pilot ship
- **Mouse**: Aim direction
- **Left Mouse Button**: Fire primary weapon
- **Right Mouse Button / E**: Activate pulse/overdrive ability
- **R**: Restart

## How to Play

- Escort 3 convoy trucks through a large scrolling world (4600x2300)
- Destroy enemies to collect ore pickups and build your score
- Chain kills to maintain a combo multiplier for bonus points
- Protect the convoy trucks from enemy fire; they have limited HP
- Use the overdrive ability when charged for devastating area attacks
- Manage your energy meter alongside health and fire cooldowns
