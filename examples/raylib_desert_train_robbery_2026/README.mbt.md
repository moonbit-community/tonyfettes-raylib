# raylib_desert_train_robbery_2026

A Wild West action-platformer where you play as an outlaw robbing a moving train across 3 chapters. Jump between wagons, shoot sheriffs, dash to dodge, and loot cargo before time runs out.

## Build and Run

```bash
cd examples && moon build --target native raylib_desert_train_robbery_2026/
cd examples && ./_build/native/debug/build/raylib_desert_train_robbery_2026/raylib_desert_train_robbery_2026.exe
```

## Controls

- **A/D or Left/Right**: Move horizontally
- **W or Up or Space**: Jump
- **J**: Shoot
- **K or Left Shift**: Dash (costs stamina)
- **L**: Loot cargo from wagon rooftops
- **R**: Restart
- Touch buttons also available on-screen

## How to Play

- Board wagon rooftops and loot cargo to meet each chapter's target
- Defeat sheriffs who patrol wagons and shoot at you
- Wagons scroll continuously; keep your balance or fall to the ground
- Ammo auto-reloads after emptying; manage stamina for dashing
- Complete all 3 chapters with escalating difficulty to win
