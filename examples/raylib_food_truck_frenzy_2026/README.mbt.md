# raylib_food_truck_frenzy_2026

A fast-paced food truck cooking and serving game. Move your chef around the truck, cook orders at stove stations, collect supply boxes, and fulfill customer orders before their patience runs out.

## Build and Run

```bash
cd examples && moon build --target native raylib_food_truck_frenzy_2026/
cd examples && ./_build/native/debug/build/raylib_food_truck_frenzy_2026/raylib_food_truck_frenzy_2026.exe
```

## Controls

- **WASD / Arrow keys**: Move chef around the truck
- **J/Space**: Interact (cook at stoves, collect supplies, serve orders)
- **K**: Boost movement speed
- **R**: Restart

## How to Play

- Customers place orders with deadlines; fulfill them before patience runs out
- Move to stove stations to cook dishes; each dish type has a cooking time and quality rating
- Collect supply boxes that spawn around the truck to keep ingredients stocked
- Customer mood drops as they wait; expired orders reduce reputation
- Manage chef stamina and heat resistance
- Earn rewards for high-quality service; chain quick deliveries for bonus points
