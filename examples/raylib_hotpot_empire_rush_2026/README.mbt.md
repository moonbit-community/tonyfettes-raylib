# Hotpot Empire Rush 2026

A kitchen management game where you run a hotpot restaurant. Navigate between stations on a grid to prepare ingredients, cook orders in the pot, and serve customers before their patience runs out.

## Build and Run

```bash
cd examples && moon build --target native raylib_hotpot_empire_rush_2026/
cd examples && ./_build/native/debug/build/raylib_hotpot_empire_rush_2026/raylib_hotpot_empire_rush_2026.exe
```

## Controls

- **W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys**: Move cursor between kitchen stations
- **J or Space**: Interact with current station / pick up / place item
- **K**: Serve or cancel at the serve counter
- **L**: Activate speed boost (uses boost meter)
- **P**: Pause
- **R**: Restart

## How to Play

Manage a 4x3 grid of kitchen stations: Broth Kettle, Protein Crate, Veg Basket, Chili Rack, Prep Board, Hotpot Base, Serve Counter, Rush Bell, Waste Bin, and more. Match incoming orders by selecting the right broth (Mala/Tomato/Mushroom), protein (Beef/Lamb/Tofu), vegetables (Lotus/Spinach/Enoki), and spice level. Prep ingredients on the board, combine them in the pot, and serve when ready. Build combos for bonus points and maintain your street reputation to stay in business.
